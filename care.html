Plant Care Guide

Cactus

Watering: Water once every 2 weeks.

Light: Needs bright sunlight.

Additional Tips: Avoid overwatering, as cacti are drought-tolerant.

Aloe Vera

Watering: Water sparingly, once every 3 weeks.

Light: Prefers indirect sunlight.

Additional Tips: Aloe Vera has healing properties, great for burns.

Peace Lily

Watering: Keep soil slightly moist.

Light: Prefers indirect light.

Additional Tips: Great for improving indoor air quality.

Fern

Watering: Needs moisture, keep soil moist.

Light: Prefers soft indirect light.

Additional Tips: Ideal for bathrooms with high humidity.

Snake Plant

Watering: Water once a week.

Light: Low to medium light.

Additional Tips: Tolerates low light conditions and is great for air purification.

Pothos

Watering: Water once a week.

Light: Prefers low light.

Additional Tips: A great plant for beginners, as it is very hardy.
